Margate Electronics
Margate Electronics is a series of curated live electronic music events. Exploring the intersection of club music and experimental electronic sound. A platform for live, hardware-focused, concept-driven performances that push creative sonic boundaries.

The project is supported using public funding by Arts Council England.
